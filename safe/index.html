SAFE — Currently (Observer Mode)

SAFE is a read-only AI market observer designed to reduce human trading error by enforcing disciplined HOLD behavior during low-conviction and high-risk market regimes.

Portfolio Note: This page is a technical portfolio demonstration of SAFE's development, testing discipline, and design philosophy. Client access to SAFE is gated and provisioned separately.

SAFE — Current Client-Facing Operating Mode (Observer)

In Observer Mode, SAFE intentionally does not:

Execute live trades
Guarantee profit or outcomes
Optimize for trade frequency or user engagement
Suppress uncertainty or low-conviction states

Design Philosophy: SAFE has demonstrated decision-generation capability via controlled paper trading. Live trade execution is intentionally disabled in client-facing mode to prioritize safety, auditability, and decision discipline.
