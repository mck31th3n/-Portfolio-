# SAFE — Public Knowledge

## What It Is

SAFE is a risk assessment engine for financial markets. It watches conditions across multiple asset types — crypto, stocks, commodities — and explains whether conditions look favorable or if it's better to wait. It does not buy, sell, or execute trades. It's an analyst, not a trader.

## Current Status

- Observer-only mode: read-only, no execution, no financial advice
- Currently being placed in a marketing domain for research purposes
- The portfolio page shows real validation data alongside sample assessments
- The actual system is deployed to the cloud

## Validated Performance (7-Day Autonomous Run)

- Processed thousands of predictions across multiple assets
- 7 out of 7 days operational, zero crashes, zero downtime
- ~95% HOLD rate — SAFE prioritizes restraint and capital preservation
- It only signals action when conditions are fully confirmed
- Observer-only throughout — no trades executed
- Continuous cloud monitoring is ongoing for research purposes

## What the HOLD Rate Means

95% of the time, SAFE says "wait." That's by design. It's built to be patient — it only signals action when everything lines up. Most systems try to trade constantly. SAFE does the opposite. It believes doing nothing is usually the smartest move.

- Not a trading bot — it does not execute trades
- Not a portfolio manager — it does not manage money
- Not financial advice — it's a research tool
- Not commercially available at this time

## Core Logic

SAFE's core decision logic is proprietary and protected. It uses standard technical indicators that any trader would recognize, but how it combines them is confidential.
