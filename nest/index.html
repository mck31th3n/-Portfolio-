Before AI can act, it submits a proposal. Guards validate it, the action is logged, and a human approves or denies. Nothing executes without a paper trail.

Owl Governance Pill
Keyword Mode

The Owl is a governance layer between AI systems and the resources they act on. It doesn't perform actions — it ensures every proposed change passes through structured validation, policy checks, and human approval before anything executes.

9 Guard Modules Validation checkpoints for every proposed action
4 Protocols Governance rules defining how decisions are made
6 Organizer Modules Proposal lifecycle from submission to resolution
35 Regression Tests Automated checks on every change

Keyword mode is deterministic — no LLM, no freeform generation. When governing, the Owl connects to an LLM for intelligent tool execution (shown below).
